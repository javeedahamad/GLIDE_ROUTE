ABOUT THE APP About Glide Route
App Overview:
 Glide Route is a comprehensive motorcycle touring companion designed for adventure riders who want to discover, plan, and share their journeys
All-in-one platform connecting the biking community with curated routes, ride planning tools, and social features
 Key Features:
 Route Discovery: Access to hundreds of verified motorcycle routes across India with difficulty ratings, seasonal recommendations, and points of interest
Interactive Maps: Detailed offline maps with elevation profiles, road conditions, and fuel stops
Community Integration: Connect with fellow riders, join group expeditions, and share your experiences
Bike Profiles: Customize your motorcycle profile with maintenance reminders and performance tracking
Weather Alerts: Real-time weather forecasts for planned routes with hazard notifications
Ride Logging: Track and record your journeys with photos, statistics, and shareable ride reports
 Technical Specifications:
 Available on iOS and Android platforms
Offline functionality for remote areas with limited connectivity
GPS integration with turn-by-turn navigation
Cloud synchronization across devices
Battery optimization for extended ride tracking
Secure user profiles and privacy controls
 Subscription Options:
 Free basic access with limited routes and features
Premium membership unlocking all routes, advanced planning tools, and unlimited ride logging
Community contribution option to earn credits by submitting verified routes
