ADVENTURES PLACES
LADHAK MUNNAR Ladakh offers an unparalleled motorcycle experience with its breathtaking high-altitude desert landscape. The Manali-Leh highway presents legendary mountain passes like Khardung La and Tanglang La, while routes to Pangong Lake and Nubra Valley reward riders with surreal blue waters and sand dunes beneath snow-capped peaks. Best visited between June and September when passes are open and weather is relatively stable. Munnar's winding roads through misty tea plantations provide a stark contrast to Ladakh's rugged terrain. The route from Kochi to Munnar via Valparai offers lush greenery, waterfalls, and wildlife encounters. The western ghats stretch provides technical riding with hairpin bends amidst cardamom plantations and spice gardens. Ideal during winter months (November-February) when rainfall is minimal and temperatures are pleasant for riding.
